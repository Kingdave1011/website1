Privacy Policy

Nexo Games | Last Updated: January 2025

Simple Summary: We collect minimal data to make the game work. Your gameplay data is saved locally and optionally online if you create an account. We don't sell your information.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary for the game to function:

  • Guest Mode: Temporary game ID, progress data (saved locally for 30 days)
  • Full Account: Username, email, gameplay progress, settings
  • Gameplay Data: Scores, unlocks, playtime, preferences
  • Technical Data: FPS, errors, crash reports (for bug fixes)
  • Optional: Feedback and bug reports you choose to send

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Save your game progress
  • Sync data across devices (if you have an account)
  • Fix bugs and improve performance
  • Show leaderboards (only if you opt-in)
  • Provide daily rewards and track playtime

3. Data Storage

Offline: Saved on your device in `user://` folder

Online: Stored securely on AWS servers (if you create an account)

Security: All online data is encrypted in transit and at rest

4. We Don't Sell or Share

Your data is never sold to third parties. We don't share your information except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law
  • To prevent fraud or abuse

5. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Request account deletion anytime
  • Export: Download your game progress
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics and error reporting

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Session management (keeping you logged in)
  • Human verification (Turnstile/CAPTCHA)
  • Remembering your settings

No third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for ages 13+. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you're a parent and believe your child provided information, contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Major changes will be announced in-game and on our website.
