How do I play Space Shooter?

Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your ship, Spacebar to shoot. Survive waves of enemies, collect power-ups, and complete missions!

What are Boosters?

Boosters are the in-game currency earned from missions, achievements, daily challenges, and the 7-day calendar rewards. Use them in the shop to unlock ships, weapons, and skins!

How many maps are there?

There are 7 unique maps: Asteroid Field, Nebula Cloud, Asteroid Belt, Space Station, Deep Space, Ice Field, and Debris Field. Each has unique visuals and difficulty levels!

Can I play with friends?

Yes! Space Shooter supports multiplayer co-op. Choose a server from 9 regional servers (32 players max per server) and team up with friends!

What do new players get?

New players receive 500 Boosters, a starter ship, basic laser weapon, and 3 shield power-ups to get started!

How do missions work?

Complete 7 different mission types with varying objectives like destroying enemies, surviving time limits, or collecting power-ups. Each mission rewards Boosters and XP!

What are the daily challenges?

A fresh challenge appears every day with special objectives. Complete them for bonus rewards! Challenges rotate through 10 different types.

How does the 7-day calendar work?

Log in each day to claim rewards! Day 1 gives 100 Boosters, building up to Day 7's Grand Prize of 1000 Boosters + exclusive ship. The calendar resets every 7 days.

Is chat moderated?

Yes! Our chat system blocks profanity, links, and spam automatically. Players who violate rules receive warnings and can be muted.

Does the game support multiple languages?

Yes! The game supports 8 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, Portuguese, and Russian. It auto-detects your system language!

When is Space Battle mode coming?

Space Battle is an upcoming game mode featuring epic fleet combat. Stay tuned for updates!

How do I unlock achievements?

Play the game! There are 13 achievements to unlock across 6 categories: Combat, Survival, Collection, Exploration, Mastery, and Secret achievements.
