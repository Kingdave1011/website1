About the Game

Galactic Combat is an intense space shooter where you battle through waves of alien invaders, collect power-ups, and fight massive bosses. Navigate through beautiful space environments including asteroid fields, nebula clouds, ice fields, and debris zones.

Features 20 unlockable maps, multiple game modes, achievements, daily challenges, and multiplayer battles with up to 32 players!
